## STEP 1 — Create the Campaign

1. Open **Google Ads** → click the blue **+ New campaign** button
2. **Campaign objective:** click **Leads**
3. **Conversion goals:** leave the defaults (phone_click and lead_form_submit should be listed). Click **Continue**
4. **Campaign type:** click **Search**. Click **Continue**
5. **Campaign name:** select the name text and replace with:

```
STC – High-Intent Local
```

6. **Networks section** — UNCHECK both:
   - ☐ Include Google Search partners
   - ☐ Include Google Display Network

7. Click **Show more settings** if needed. Leave Ad rotation on "Optimize"

---

## STEP 2 — Bidding

1. Under **Bidding**, click "Or, select a bid strategy directly"
2. Choose **Maximize conversions**
3. Leave "Target CPA" BLANK (we'll add one after 2 weeks of data)
