Veteran Home Care — Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions from veterans and their families in South Florida.

What is VA Aid & Attendance?

VA Aid & Attendance is a monthly pension benefit for wartime veterans (and surviving spouses) who need help with daily activities. In 2026, it provides up to $2,424/month for a single veteran, or $2,874/month for a veteran with one dependent. These funds can be used to pay for in-home care services like the ones we provide.

Am I eligible for VA Aid & Attendance benefits?

You may qualify if you: (1) served during a wartime period, (2) need help with daily activities like bathing, feeding, or dressing, (3) are housebound due to disability, or (4) have limited eyesight. You must also meet income/net worth limits — currently $163,699 through November 2026. Surviving spouses of eligible veterans may also qualify.

Can VA benefits really pay for my home care?

Yes! VA Aid & Attendance benefits can cover in-home care services including companionship, personal care, medication reminders, and even 24/7 live-in care. At ~$32/hour for care in Palm Beach County, your monthly VA benefit of $2,424+ can cover approximately 15-18 hours of weekly care at little to no personal cost.

How long does the VA Aid & Attendance application take?

Processing times vary, but typically take 3-6 months. The good news: we can begin providing care immediately while your application is being processed. We'll help gather the necessary documentation and can connect you with accredited VSOs to strengthen your claim.

Do you provide transportation to VA appointments?

Yes. Our caregivers can provide transportation assistance to the Thomas H. Corey VA Medical Center on N Military Trail, the Palm Beach Vet Center in Greenacres, or any other VA clinic or medical appointment in the Palm Beach and Broward County area.

What areas do you serve for veteran home care?

We serve veterans across 44+ communities in Palm Beach County and Broward County, including West Palm Beach, Boca Raton, Jupiter, Palm Beach Gardens, Wellington, Delray Beach, Boynton Beach, Fort Lauderdale, Coral Springs, Pompano Beach, and many more. See the full list of service areas below.
