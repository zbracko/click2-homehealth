## 📊 KEY LIVE DATA INSIGHTS INFORMING THIS STRUCTURE

1. **TheKey dominates paid #1 position** across ALL major queries — they bid aggressively with science-based messaging and sitelinks. STC should NOT try to outbid them; instead, differentiate on "local, family-owned, no contracts."

2. **"24 hour home care" has lower paid competition** than generic "home care" — Focus Family Care and Executive Home Care rank organically, but few paid ads. MASSIVE opportunity for STC.

3. **"Memory care at home" is a real business name** (Memory Care At Home, 700 S Rosemary Ave, WPB) with only 1 Google review. STC can win this keyword space easily.

4. **Respite care has almost ZERO paid competition** — only organic results from nonprofits (Catholic Charities, Salvation Army, Arc of PBC). STC can own this ad space at very low CPC.

5. **Veteran home care: ZERO competitors** in live SERPs. This is a completely untapped micro-niche.

6. **Visiting Angels has 150 reviews** (most in market) — STC needs to build review count fast to compete in Local Pack.

7. **Pricing signals in SERPs:** Visiting Angels quotes $31.71/hr and $5,400-$6,400/week for 24-hour care. STC should use similar or better pricing in ad copy.

8. **AgingCare.com and Care.com** are aggregator/marketplace competitors — they capture research-phase traffic. STC's audience pages (aging-in-place, elderly-care) can intercept this traffic.

9. **"People Also Ask" questions** should be added as FAQ content on landing pages for Quality Score improvement and potential featured snippet capture.

10. **Broward County shows Aqua Home Care (Fort Lauderdale)** and Accessible Home Health Care of Broward as primary competitors — different competitive landscape than Palm Beach County.
