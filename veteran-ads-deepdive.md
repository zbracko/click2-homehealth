## 🎯 AUDIENCE SEGMENTATION DEEP-DIVE

### Primary Audience: Veteran Decision-Makers (3 Personas)

#### Persona 1: The Aging Veteran (Direct)
- **Age:** 70-95
- **Wars:** Korea, Vietnam, Gulf War
- **Behavior:** Searches "VA benefits for home care", "VA Aid & Attendance"
- **Device:** Desktop 60%, Tablet 25%, Mobile 15%
- **Search times:** Morning (7-10am), after VA appointments
- **Pain:** Needs help but doesn't know benefits exist, pride/independence
- **Key trigger:** Hospitalization, fall, spouse death, doctor recommendation

#### Persona 2: The Adult Child of a Veteran
- **Age:** 40-65
- **Behavior:** Searches "home care for veteran father", "VA pays for home care", "help for aging veteran parent"
- **Device:** Mobile 55%, Desktop 45%
- **Search times:** Evening (7-10pm), lunch break (12-2pm), weekends
- **Pain:** Caregiver burnout, guilt, doesn't know about VA benefits, cost anxiety
- **Key trigger:** Parent declining, can't manage alone, researching after crisis

#### Persona 3: The Veteran's Spouse
- **Age:** 65-85
- **Behavior:** Searches "help for husband veteran", "surviving spouse VA benefits"
- **Device:** Desktop 50%, Mobile 30%, Tablet 20%
- **Search times:** Daytime hours
- **Pain:** Exhausted from caregiving, doesn't know about surviving spouse benefits
- **Key trigger:** Can't physically care for spouse anymore, own health declining
